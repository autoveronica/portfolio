Speed Performance Optimisation & Website Migration

Speed Performance Optimisation & Website Migration at Sellics

Industry: SaaS | Location: Berlin | My role: Marketing Designer & Product Manager

I led the end-to-end website migration for Sellics, transitioning from Unbounce and HubSpot to a streamlined WordPress solution. Applying product management principles, I scoped requirements, prioritised features using a value vs. complexity model, and coordinated cross-functional teams to enhance site performance and design consistency. I created a lightweight design system using ACF components, rebuilt all landing pages, optimised loading times by removing heavy plugins, and developed a reusable icon font to empower the marketing team.

Post-migration, the website's GTmetrix speed rating improved from Grade F to B, with analytics tools like Google Analytics and Hotjar indicating increased user engagement through more clicks, taps, and scrolling behaviours. The visual presentation of the brand achieved consistency, and the conversion rate saw a notable increase, reflecting the positive impact of the migration on user experience and business outcomes.
